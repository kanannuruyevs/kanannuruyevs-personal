About

I am a Content Creator with a background in IT and programming, now focusing on creating engaging content for social media platforms to attract and retain followers. I am actively seeking career opportunities that align with my evolving passions and strengths. My dedication to continuous learning and collaboration enables me to adapt and tackle challenges in any field I pursue.

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Information Technology, earned in 2020. My journey began with programming, starting with Python and focusing on Backend Development. Later, I transitioned to Frontend Development, working with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React.js. However, after experiencing burnout, I decided to pivot my career path.

Currently, I am channeling my skills into content creation and social media management. This new direction allows me to leverage my problem-solving and analytical abilities in a creative and impactful way. I am excited to see where this journey takes me.
